Privacy Policy for Code Blue

Last Updated: June 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Code Blue Discord bot ("we", "our", or "the bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the bot on Discord.

1. Information We Collect

Code Blue may collect the following information to provide its features:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Server (Guild) IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Command usage logs
  • Message content (only when required for a feature, not stored long-term)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Operate and maintain bot features
  • Respond to user commands
  • Track usage to improve performance
  • Prevent abuse or misuse of the bot

3. Data Retention

We only retain data for as long as it is necessary to operate the bot. Most data (e.g., logs) are temporary and purged regularly. We do not store any personal messages or user-generated content unless explicitly needed for functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Data is never sold, leased, or disclosed unless required by law or for security reasons (e.g., bot abuse or Discord ToS violations).

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Request the removal of your data
  • Inquire about what data is stored (if any)
  • Report misuse or abuse

To make a request, contact us through the support server linked below.

6. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the bot implies your acceptance of any changes.
